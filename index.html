Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Bern (He/Him)

Hey there! Welcome to this corner of the internet where you learn things about me!

I am Panayiotis, a computer scientist who develops systems with societal impact and enjoys interdisciplinary collaborations. I have expertise in applying Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning methods to texts with heterogeneous formality, intent, and writing style, such as social and news media, interview transcripts, and scientific publications.

Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Bern (UniBE) exploring the usage of Large Language Models in Sociology. I obtained my PhD degree from the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), and my MSc and BSc degrees from the University of Athens (UoA).
